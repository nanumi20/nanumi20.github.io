How to play

How to play

This is how you play the game

Deliver requested food

Once the game starts, each household will ask you for different dish option. Deliver the correct dish by dragging them and earn 20 points. If you score higher than the mission, then you clear the stage! Yay!

Don't make a mistake

If you deliver food that was not requested by the household, then you will lose 10 points. Also, the people who receive the food will be unhappy :(

It gets harder

From stage 2, people gets more hungry, and the households might ask you for more than one dish. Also, the mission goal gets higher, so speed up your deliveries!

GAME OVER

When the time (30 seconds) runs out before you clear the mission, then the game will be over.. Try again to score higher!
